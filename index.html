About

I work on nonlinear PDEs arising in fluid/plasma models: hyperbolic conservation laws, kinetic theory, and stability/singularity of nonlinear waves.

PDE Hyperbolic conservation laws Kinetic theory Nonlinear waves Singularity & stability

Professional

  • Full Professor, UNIST (2023–present)
  • Associate Professor, UNIST (2016–2022)
  • Assistant Professor, UNIST (2012–2016)
  • Visiting Assistant Professor, Texas A&M University (2009–2012)
  • Ph.D., Mathematics, Indiana University Bloomington (2009)

Research themes

Singularity formation & sharp regularity

Gradient blow-up, self-similar dynamics, refined asymptotics.

Stability of nonlinear waves

Shock profiles, solitary waves, spectral/energy methods.

Plasma / Euler–Poisson / NSP

Quasi-neutral limits, boundary layers, long-time behavior.

Kinetic & interacting particle models

Vlasov-type PDEs, collective dynamics, fluid coupling.
